Puppetmaster

Enables create-unit and change-control for spectators, which can act as puppet-masters over the poor players.

alt+ctrl+shift+p - Toggle puppetmaster (spectators only)
ctrl+v - Paste unit. Select a player and a unit from the extra panels in puppetmaster mode.
shift+ctrl+v - Paste 10 units.

Extra keybindings should be configurable if you visit Settings while in a modded game.

Since you have to change control to give a player units, the mod also allows the puppet-master to give unit orders, including self-destruct.
Extra commanders don't change the losing conditions - only original coms count for assassination.
Controlling a player stops army stat updates; the mod clears control flags when the spectator panel opened, but it can be easy to forget you need to re-select a player in order to paste.
